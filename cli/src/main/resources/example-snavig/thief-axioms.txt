"Resource" import "/snavig/possession-axioms.txt".

A thief is a kind of non-player-character.
A thief must be alive or dead.
A thief must be boorish or suave.

After a game-character kills a kill-victim,
 the kill-victim is dead.
After a game-character kills a thief,
 the game-interpreter says "Blood splatters everywhere.".

When a game-character throws a game-weapon,
 the game-character drops it.
When a game-character throws a game-weapon at a throw-target,
 the game-character kills the throw-target with the game-weapon.

There is a thief.
Whenever the game-turn advances,
 the thief surveys the thief's container.
