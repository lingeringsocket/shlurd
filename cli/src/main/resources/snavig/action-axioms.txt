"Resource" import "/snavig/interpreter-axioms.txt".

When a player-character looks at a describable-entity,
 the player-character examines the describable-entity.

When a player-character looks,
 the player-character appraises the player-character's container.

When a player-character discovers a map-place,
 the player-character appraises the map-place.

When a player-character appraises a map-place,
 the player-character asks "where am I";
 also the game-interpreter recites the map-place's place-description;
 also the player-character discerns the map-place's spc-contained-objects.

When a player-character rediscovers a map-place,
 the player-character asks "where am I";
 also the player-character discerns the map-place's spc-contained-objects.

// this applies only if at least one visible-entity is discerned
When a player-character discerns a visible-entity,
 the player-character asks "what do I see".

When a player-character examines a describable-entity,
 the describable-entity's description might exist;
 otherwise the game-interpreter says "It's about what you'd expect.".
When a player-character examines a describable-entity,
 the game-interpreter recites the describable-entity's description.
